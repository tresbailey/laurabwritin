Embracing Eternity in the Here and Now

Exhausted. Maxed Out. Running on Fumes. Bone-Tired. Drowning.

We women long for margin, rest, and affirmation that all of our efforts mean something. Surely, there must be a purpose beyond this daily grind that overwhelms our schedules and our hearts.

What’s the answer for today’s worn-out woman? A shift in perspective.

In this book, we will journey through Ecclesiastes and discover how living with an eternal perspective results in more peaceful, purposeful, and plentiful lives today. Through 21 devotions, rich in theology and application, this book will help you:

  • Discover how to cling to an eternal perspective in all seasons and situations
  • Determine how to evaluate the worth of your time and energy
  • Dive into scripture for timeless reminders about true freedom
